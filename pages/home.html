Show Me More

rosehip is an events management and consultation company that specializes in corporate events and day of coordinating. Owner, Marisa Marquez, can turn your corporate event into a bespoke dynamic moment in time for your clients and colleagues to enjoy.

After successfully building the events department in the U.S. for Allen & Overy, a global corporate law firm, Marisa decided to venture out on her own and founded rosehip. While at Allen & Overy she executed numerous events from small dinners to thousand person advanced screenings of Star Wars.

Over her career she has worked with Disney, The Public Theater, W-Hotel, Marriott, AMC, SIFMA, and managed events in New York, Denver, San Diego, Orlando, and Miami. Her background in theatrical and film production gives her events an edge while paying close care to all the logistical details.
